Market Value for gilts and DBV collateral shall be determined solely by reference to

Default Valuation Time, with the calculation notified by Default Valuation Notice

**(b) Modified Act of Insolvency.** Paraphrase: the petition limb applies only if the

reflecting the manager's restructuring flexibility).

para 10(a)(i) unless the failure continues for three business days after notice.

**(d) Set-off rider.** Paraphrase: Party A may set off any amounts owed by Party B or
any of Party B's affiliates under any agreement against sums due under this

**(e) Fish-or-cut-bait rider (fund markup, added by Amendment Letter No. 2, 2013).**
Paraphrase: if Party A withholds performance following a potential Event of Default

**(f) Default interest rider.** Paraphrase: amounts unpaid after default accrue at
GBP LIBOR plus 200bp, compounded daily by reference to ISMA conventions.

**(g) Substitution pre-agreement.** Paraphrase: Party A consents in advance to
substitution of Purchased Securities of the same issuer class up to twice per month,
valuation as at the substitution date.
